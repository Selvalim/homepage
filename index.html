Xiaolin Wen 温啸林

I am now a research engineer and a PhD student at Singapore Management University under the guidance of Prof. Yong Wang. In 2023, I got my master degree in computer science at Vision Computing Lab, College of Computer Sicence, Sichuan University, and my supervisor was Prof. Min Zhu. In 2020, I got my dual bachelor degree in computer science and finance at Sichuan University.

My research interests include Human-Computer Interaction and Data Visualization.
